# Graph Algorithms I can eventually leverage

As your graph grows, standard graph algorithms become useful:

|Algorithm|Use in your app|
|---|---|
|Breadth-First Search (BFS)|Find all concepts closely related to the current node.|
|Depth-First Search (DFS)|Follow a complete engineering workflow or dependency chain.|
|Shortest Path (Dijkstra/A*)|Explain how two concepts are connected.|
|Topological Sort|Determine the correct calculation order when dependencies exist.|
|Connected Components|Detect isolated or incomplete sections of your knowledge base.|
|PageRank|Surface the most influential engineering concepts (e.g., Pressure, Material, Temperature).|
|Community Detection|Automatically group nodes into areas like Piping, Vessels, Materials, Inspection, Welding.|
